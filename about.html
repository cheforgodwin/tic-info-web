About Us

Welcome to the About Us page of the TIC Foundation. Here you can learn more about our mission, vision, and the impact we are making in the tech community.

About TiC

  • Founded in 2020 to foster tech innovation in Africa.
  • Mission: Empower young innovators with skills, mentorship, and resources.
  • Milestones: First hackathon in 2023.
  • Founder: Bill Agha

Programs Overview

  • Hackathons: Annual competitions for devs, designers, entrepreneurs.
  • Youth Labs: STEM training sessions for students.
  • Mentorship: One-on-one guidance from professionals.
  • Internships: Hands-on sessions on various tech topics (e.g., web development, data science,cyber security).

Success Stories

  • 300+ alumni have launched successful startups.
  • Manfred Asah Agha, a young Cameroonian innovator, created the Intelligent Eye Glass for the Blind to aid visually impaired individuals. In 2025, he won the Special Prize of the President of the Republic with a 10 million FCFA award. He plans to use the funds to develop 20 prototypes to help over 6,000 people in Cameroon..
